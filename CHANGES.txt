1.0.0
	New Stuff:
	- Output is now available when using the /hmq command.
	- Added a delay (0.1s) to the quest automation to reduce the likelihood of the addon getting "stuck".
	- Any mod key can now be used to pause the automation.
	- The selection of Threads of Fate is now entirely automated.
	
	Bug Fixes:
	- Fixed an issue causing incomplete quests to take precedence over completed ones that were further down the list.
